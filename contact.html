Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a viewing?

The easiest way is to click the WhatsApp button on any listing page. You can also use the contact form above, and our agent will reach out within 24 hours.

Are bills included in the price?

Check the "Bills Included" badge on each listing. For most of our managed properties, utilities, high-speed WiFi, and water are included.

Is there a booking fee?

No, we do not charge students any direct booking or administration fees. Any security deposits go directly to the property manager or landlord.
